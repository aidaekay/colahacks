ABOUT US

ColaHacks is an hackathon hosted at the University of South Carolina in Columbia, SC. Students from USC and other universities will come together to bring their ideas to life in the form of web, mobile, hardware, and any development. Students will have 12 hours to learn, build, and have fun in a fast-paced yet collaborative environment. Participants will compete to show judges why their project makes a difference for the chance to win awards and prizes.
